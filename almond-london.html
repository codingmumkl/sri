Almond London Recipes

INGREDIENTS:

  • 100 gr all purpose flour
  • 125 gr corn flour
  • 1 tbsp rice flour
  • 125 gr unsalted butter
  • 75 gr icing sugar
  • 125 gr corn flour
  • 125 gr corn flour
  • 2 tbsp powdered milk
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 25 gr cornflakes
  • 70 toasted whole almonds , aprox. 85 gr
    • For the coating:
  • 400 gr chocolate , minimum 55% cocoa
  • 8 gr unsalted butter
  • almond nibs as needed
  • Almond London Cookies

INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. In a bowl, sift the all purpose flour, rice flour and corn flour. Whisk everything together and set aside
  2. In another bowl, add in butter and icing sugar. Mix just until well combined.
    Add in powdered milk and mix again just to combine.
    Add in egg yolk, vanilla extract and again mix just to combine.
  3. Add in blended cornflakes and mix to combine. While still mixing,
    lower the speed of the mixer and add in flour mixture bit by bit,
    mixing just to combine as you go. Form the dough into a ball,
    cover with cling film when not using.
  4. Take a pice of dough, flatten it a bit and place one almond in the center.
    Cover it with the dough and shape it into an oval log that is more or less thumb sized.
    Repeat for the rest of the dough. Place them on a baking tray with baking sheet.>
  5. Bake at 170ºC (340ºF) preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or
    until the bottom edges are golden brown and they're fully
    cooked. Cool completely on a rack, around 40 minutes.
    6. The chocolate coating:
  6. Melt the chocolate and butter using double boiler method.

    RECIPE NOTES....

  1. Over mixing the butter and sugar will make the cookies expand,
    which we don't want in this recipe.
  2. Over mixing the flour will make the cookies hard, which we also don't want.
